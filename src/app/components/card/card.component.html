This page lets you save your selected card to a deck and add land to said deck.

Ideally, decks contain 20-24 land. Depending on the mana cost, you may have more or less than this.

Type
Discerns the type of card or creature it is. You should consider adding cards of similar type to the same deck for synergies in-game. E.g. One would add many vampires to a vampire deck.
Mana Cost
A deck should ideally contain a large amount of 3 or 4 cost cards, a lower number of 1 and 2 cost cards, and a lower number of 5-6+ cost cards. Converted Mana Cost is the total number of mana needed to play a card. (4)(B)(B) means you need 4 of any colour mana to play it and 2 blue mana.
Rarity
The rarities include Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Mythic Rare. The higher rarity a card is, the more powerful it will be, though it may also have a higher mana cost.
